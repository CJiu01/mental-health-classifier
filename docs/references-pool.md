# Reference Pool (TP2 후보 논문)

## 기존 TP2 초안에서 사용하던 논문 5편

1. **De Choudhury, Counts & Horvitz (2013)**
   Social media as a measurement tool of depression in populations. *WebSci 2013.*
   https://doi.org/10.1145/2464464.2464480
   → 텍스트 기반 정신건강 신호 탐지의 원조 동기 논문

2. **Coppersmith, Dredze, Harman, Hollingshead & Mitchell (2015)**
   CLPsych 2015 shared task: Depression and PTSD on Twitter. *CLPsych.*
   https://aclanthology.org/W15-1204/
   → 벤치마크 스타일 평가 (Acc, Macro F1, 클래스별 분석) 근거

3. **Belcastro et al. (2025)**
   Detecting mental disorder on social media: a ChatGPT-augmented explainable approach. *Online Social Networks and Media.*
   https://arxiv.org/abs/2401.17477
   → 빠른 분류기 + LLM 설명 조합 설계 근거 (Ch.4/Ch.11 + Ch.6)

4. **Kim, Imieye & Yin (2025)**
   Interpretable depression detection from social media text using LLM-derived embeddings. *arXiv:2506.06616.*
   https://arxiv.org/abs/2506.06616
   → SentenceTransformer 임베딩 + 지도 분류기 설계 지지

5. **Ge et al. (2025)**
   Survey and experiments on mental disorder detection via social media: From LLMs and RAG to agents. *arXiv:2504.02800.*
   https://arxiv.org/abs/2504.02800
   → RAG, 신뢰성, 안전성 등 미래 방향 논의 근거
